What is an Atom?

Atoms are really tiny particles which make everything in the universe. They are the smallest unit of matter and form chemical elements. There are many types of atoms with different properties which such as carbon or oxygen atoms. We are made up of 7 billion billion billion atoms. They are in short the building blocks of everything.
